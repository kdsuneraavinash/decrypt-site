GAMEZONE

What is GameZone?

GameZone is an e-gaming competition to make a fun and relaxing change in your monotonous life. With a variety of your favourite PC and Xbox games, it will surely capture the heart and soul of any gaming enthusiast!


What are the games included?

Call Of Duty 4
NFS Most Wanted
Mortal Combat


Who can participate?

Teams consisting of 5 players. All players should be students of University of Moratuwa.


Why should you participate?

Do we really have to explain that? Because you love gaming, duh!


What do you get by participating?

This will be an amazing opportunity for you to relax and restore your mental health. You also get to test your gaming skills against your peers. Winning GameZone will definitely give you a well earned right to boast! And of course, exciting cash prizes await the winners!


PS: A little bit of practicing beforehand will not do you any harm.. It's not always that you get such a good excuse to keep gaming!

GENERAL PLAYER RULES

  1. Participants should provide their University ID at the event to confirm their registration.
  2. Flash drives/thumb drives are prohibited from playing area. If a player is found to plug in a flash drive to a playing pc during a match or in between, that team is disqualified from the tournament.
  3. For custom cfgs players must get prior approval from match officials before downloading/copying/installing. Failure to do so would result in a disqualification.
  4. Gaming Mouse/Keyboards/Head Phones are allowed. Players themselves are responsible for their property and GZ staff or match officials would not be responsible for damages and losses.
  5. Players must setup their hardware 10 mins before a match. (Only plug & Play devices are allowed)
  6. No swearing in-game and within the gaming area.
  7. Organizers have the full liberty to change the rules as per situation and players must follow them accordingly.
  8. Spectators/ Substitutes must remain silent during a match and should not interfere with players.
  9. All the games are restricted for teams of University of Moratuwa students

COD4

1. - GAME MODE SETTINGS

  • BOMB TIMER: 45 SECONDS
  • DEFUSE TIME: 7 SECONDS
  • PLANT TIME: 5 SECONDS
  • ROUND LIMIT: 12 ROUNDS, WITH HALF TIME AND SWITCHING SIDES AT 6
  • TIME LIMIT: 1.45 MINUTES
  • SCORE LIMIT: FIRST TEAM TO REACH 7 WILL BE DEFINED MAP WINNER
  • ALL MATCHES WILL BE PLAYED ON PROMODLIVE2.20 EU (PROMOD_MODE LAN_KNOCKOUT_MR6).

2. - BANNED WEAPONS, ATTACHMENTS AND GRENADES

  • P90
  • Scorpion
  • M21
  • Dragunov
  • Barrett
  • .50cal
  • LMG
  • All Attachments are banned except for silencers.
Note: for Special Grenades, the stun grenade is disabled. All perks are disabled.

3. - CLASSES

A team with five players can use a maximum of:
One (1) Sniper
One (1) Demolition
Two (2) SMG
Five (5) Assault

4. - MAPS

MP_BACKLOT
MP_CRASH
MP_CROSSFIRE
MP_CITYSTREET
MP_STRIKE

5. - SIDE SELECTION

Side selection will be decided by a coin toss on each map where the winner will decide whether to attack or defend first.

6. - NUMBER OF MAPS

Only one map will be played and that map decided by removing of maps from the pool of 5 official maps until only one map remains. Each team gets to remove two maps from the pool. A coin toss will decide who removes the first map (Loser of the coin toss removes the first map so the winner gets the chance to remove the last map from the remaining two maps).

7. - CALLING A WIN

FIRST TO REACH 7 ROUNDS WINS THE MATCH.

8. - TIE BREAKER

If both teams have an equal number of map wins at the end, a tie breaker map will be played where the winner of that map will win the match. The tie breaker map will be selected by a knife round where winners pick the map. Again the sides for the map will be decided by a knife round and the number of rounds reduced to mr3. If the first tie breaker ends up as a draw same map will be played and sides will be changed (e.g.: first tie breaker match team who attacked first now have to defend first).

9. - OTHER RULES

  1. Plugging in USB storage devices to tournament PCs is strictly prohibited.
  2. Permitted Bindings include;- Changes to assigned stock single key game play binds.- Binding
    3. of not more than two motion keys (Example: bind a +leanright;+moveright)- If you have a non-stock binding that you're unsure is legal, or have a binding that you would like to have approved for use in match play, submit the information to an admin for clarification and/or consideration.- Namebinds- Demoscripts- Weapon/-menu/-select-Scripts (includes all grenades)- Volume binds/toggles- Any player found using illegal binds will be subject to possible removal from the competition.
  3. Banned exploits-Map exploits defined:Map exploits are errors or oversights in map design made by the map developer that provide an unfair advantage to a player or a team.Such exploits include but are not limited to:- the ability to lean through, pass through, and see through walls, doors, and windows, and use of clipped or defective map passages to access areas outside the normal playing area. Any player, group of players, or team proven to be using illegal map exploits or boosting will be subject to loss of match points and possible expulsion from the Tournament. Anyone found cheating will face a match forfeit and/or removal of team from the competition.
  4. Substitutions - Only players signed up on the team list may be used as substitutes

MORTAL COMBAT

Rules

  • 1v1 knockout game.
  • All players have second chance to compete (Loosers bracket).
  • Winner take by 2/3 (Best of three),Final 8 goes 3/5 (best of five).
  • Only looser can change the character.
  • Before official match start there will be a button check round.
  • After match was started any player cant pause the game.
  • If the game was paused or under any reasonable issue match should be restarted.
