IDEATHON

What is Ideathon?

Decrypt Ideathon is a platform for pitching your creative solutions for existing problems. The competition is focused on enhancing the entrepreneurial skills of freshmen and pushing them forward on their journey towards excellence. Along the way, the applicants will be provided with opportunities for training and mentoring to sharpen their analytical, innovative, pitching and many more skills to perfection.


Who can participate?

Ideathon is an opportunity exclusively available for students of the '18 Batch to showcase their skills. Groups consisting of 3-4 engineering faculty students of the '18 Batch of University of Moratuwa can participate in the competition.


Why should you participate?

Ideathon could be your first experience in entrepreneurship. Participants get the golden opportunity to develop the skill of providing creative solutions for prevailing problems with existing technology. The finalists get professional training in collaboration with leading companies in Sri Lanka to experience the industry as well as to enhance their skills and personality.


What do you get by participating?

Apart from a valuable opportunity to experience the corporate world and polish your skills.. exciting cash prizes will be awarded to winners!
